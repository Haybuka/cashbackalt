What is 9ja Cashback

9ja CashBack is an Electronic Transaction Reward Service ( ETRS) that scores and rewards users with refunds and giveaway for electronic financial transactions they register with the service. Basically after making successful financial transaction e.g ATM withdrawal, POS payments ,online bill payment etc users can register such transactions by requesting for cashback and have their wishes come true.

9jaCashBack is available to every ATM debit card holder in Nigeria and can be accessed directly from any ATM, web , mobile and USSD
